Naruto was born as the son of the Fourth Hokage, Minato Namikaze, and Kushina Uzumaki.
However, shortly after his birth, the Nine-Tailed Fox (Kurama) was sealed inside him to save the village from destruction.
This made him an outcast in the Hidden Leaf Village, as many people feared and resented him due to the destruction caused by Kurama.
He grew up lonely, without parents, and constantly seeking the acknowledgment of others.

Throughout his journey as a ninja, Naruto faced many challenges and made powerful friends and enemies.
He fought against dangerous rogue ninjas, including members of the Akatsuki, an organization of powerful ninjas who sought to capture the tailed beasts like Kurama.
Naruto's bond with Sasuke became central to the series, as Sasuke eventually left the village to seek power and revenge, leading Naruto to pursue him.
